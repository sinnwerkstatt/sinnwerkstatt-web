### Word Wrapping

Force word wrapping:

* Add CSS: word-wrap:break-word; -moz-hyphens: auto;
* Place [zero-width spaces](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zero_Width_Space) (<code>& # 8203 ;</code>) at the points where you want to allow breaks.
